Why it fits your program

Improve SRTR outcomes

Connecting patients to assistance before cost becomes a barrier directly supports graft survival and your program's SRTR performance.

Reduce readmissions

Patients who can't afford immunosuppressants end up back in the hospital. Financial navigation at discharge reduces cost-driven non-adherence.

Support CMS compliance

CMS Conditions of Participation require documented patient education and financial planning. This is a trackable, standardized resource.
