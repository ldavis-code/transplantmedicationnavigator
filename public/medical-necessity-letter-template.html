Risk of Non-Approval

  • Acute rejection episode requiring hospitalization and potentially additional immunosuppression
  • Chronic rejection leading to graft loss and need for re-transplantation or return to dialysis
  • Drug toxicity from unstable blood levels
  • Increased healthcare costs from complications far exceeding the cost difference between medications
