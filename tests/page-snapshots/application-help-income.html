Who Can Get Help

Most drug companies publish clear income guidelines. Here's what you need to know:

Don't Count Yourself Out: Many programs have much higher income limits than you might expect.

How Income Limits Work

  • Based on Federal Poverty Level (FPL) percentages
  • Vary by household size
  • Updated annually with FPL changes

Where to Find Guidelines

  • Manufacturer's website
  • MAT.org search results
  • Call the program directly
