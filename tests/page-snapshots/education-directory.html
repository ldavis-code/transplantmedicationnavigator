TotalAssist Is Here, the Nation's Largest Charitable Patient Assistance Program

The Patient Advocate Foundation and PAN Foundation have merged to launch TotalAssist, the nation's largest charitable patient assistance program. Over a combined 50 years, the two organizations have helped 3.8 million patients and granted over $7 billion in financial assistance.

As of July 1, 2026, TotalAssist combines all financial assistance into one place, with nearly 150 disease-specific and health equity funds, no wait lists, and instant eligibility decisions in most cases. Grants help cover medication copays, coinsurance, deductibles, insurance premiums, office visit charges, and treatment-related costs. Eligibility generally extends to patients with income at or below 500% of the Federal Poverty Level, and a six-month lookback can reimburse recent expenses.

Apply online or call 866-512-3861 (Mon–Fri, 8:30am–5:30pm ET; press 2 for Spanish).

Trusted Resource Directory

American Kidney Fund (AKF)

Foundation

Gives money to help dialysis and transplant patients pay for treatment if they qualify.

American Transplant Foundation

Foundation

Gives grants to help patients pay for medicines. Also links transplant patients with mentors who've been through the same thing.

Cost Plus Drugs

Low-Cost Pharmacy

A low-cost online pharmacy with clear, low prices. Good for comparing cash prices to what you pay with insurance.

Donate Life America

Support Group

Connects transplant patients with local resources, events, and support groups across the United States.

Drugs.com

Medication Database

Look up medicines, find who makes them, and connect to their help programs.

GoodRx

Discount Program

Free drug discount coupons. Compare prices at different pharmacies and get coupons right away.

HealthWell Foundation

Foundation

Helps patients who don't have enough insurance coverage. Pays for copays, travel costs, and insurance premiums.

Heartfelt Help Foundation

Foundation

Helps heart transplant patients in Northern California who need money help. Pays for housing near the hospital and other costs insurance doesn't cover.

Medicare.gov

Government

The official U.S. government site for Medicare. Compare drug plans and check if you qualify for help.

National Alliance for Caregiving

Caregiver Support

A group that helps family caregivers. Offers tips, info, and support for people caring for loved ones.

NORD RareCare

Foundation

Helps pay for life-saving medications, insurance premiums, and copays. Also helps with testing for rare diseases.

TotalAssist by Patient Advocate Foundation (formerly PAF + PAN Foundation)

Foundation

Launched July 1, 2026 after PAF and PAN Foundation merged, TotalAssist is the nation's largest charitable patient assistance program. Nearly 150 disease-specific and health equity funds help pay for copays, coinsurance, deductibles, insurance premiums, and treatment costs, with no wait lists, instant eligibility in most cases, and a six-month lookback. Apply at portal.totalassist.org or call 866-512-3861.

PAN Foundation FundFinder

Foundation Directory

A list of over 150 patient groups that offer helplines, support, and education for specific diseases.

Accessia Health (formerly Patient Services Inc)

Foundation

Helps pay for medications and insurance costs. Also helps you understand your insurance and gives legal advice.

PhRMA's Medicine Assistance Tool (MAT)

Search Engine

PhRMA's patient assistance resources. It is not a program itself, but helps you find patient assistance programs offered by drug companies.

Rx Outreach

Low-Cost Pharmacy

A nonprofit pharmacy that sells medicines at low prices. Has saved patients lots of money.

TRIO (Transplant Recipients International Organization)

Support Group

The largest group for transplant patients, donors, and their families. Offers local chapters, mentoring, and learning resources.
