Important Safety Note for Transplant Patients

Never switch from brand name to generic (or between different generic manufacturers) without your transplant team's approval. In transplant medicine, slight variations in bioavailability can cause organ rejection. "Cheaper" isn't always "safe" if the manufacturer changes.
