The challenge

Cost is the leading modifiable driver of graft loss: about 40% of transplant recipients skip doses because of cost (AST Therapeutic Needs Study, 2025). At [Center Name], coordinators needed a fast, standardized way to connect patients to copay cards and assistance programs at discharge, without adding PHI, accounts, or IT burden.

What we deployed

Transplant Medication Navigator was offered as branded patient education via [discharge QR code / MyChart / branded URL]. Patients searched their medications, imported their list from Epic MyChart, and were matched to copay cards, patient assistance programs, and foundation grants across 218 transplant medications. No PHI collected; no BAA required.
